Welcome to Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station in the Himalayas, known for its scenic beauty and pleasant weather. It's a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Best Places in Manali

Hadimba Temple - A peaceful place surrounded by cedar trees, rich in history and beauty.

Solang Valley - Famous for skiing, paragliding, and other adventurous activities.

Rohtang Pass - A must-visit spot for breathtaking views and snow-capped mountains.

Manikaran - Known for its hot springs and Gurudwara, a spiritual and serene place.

Old Manali - A charming village with cozy cafes, vibrant markets, and unique culture.

Vashisht Temple - A tranquil place known for its natural hot water springs.

Jogini Waterfalls - A scenic trail leading to a beautiful cascading waterfall.

Beas River - A picturesque spot for relaxing and enjoying the serenity of nature.

Naggar Castle - A historical castle offering stunning views of the valley.
